This is lightweight auto clicker made by 22Eustachy (EustaczTheDev), that supports mouse and keyboard buttons, custom click invertals
and even click offset. Inspired by OP Auto Clicker 4.1 (https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/XPFM2K6GPV72CR?hl=pl-PL&gl=PL&ocid=pdpshare)
but supports keybord buttons.
